Biography

After being rejected from music school twice, Seth Elgin took matters into his own hands, releasing "Running Out of Days" on March 1, 2026. While he began writing the song in high school, he shelved it for years unable to find quite the right words to finish it. Then, facing a bout of anxiety in the tail end of 2025 about his career, schooling, and life in general, he turned back to the song and prepared it for release. "Ain't That Just the Way" followed soon after as a love letter to the early 2000's punk-rock he grew up on. With other songs on the horizon, keep your eyes on Seth Elgin; his story is far from over.
